How to Play Memory

Game Rules

  • The board consists of a multitude of pairs of matching cards, like these:
  • When the game begins, all cards are placed face down
  • Players now take turns flipping two cards at a time
  • If the cards match, the player keeps them and gets another turn, but if they don't, they get flipped back down and the next Player takes their turn.
  • The game ends when all pairs are found
  • The player with the most pairs wins!
