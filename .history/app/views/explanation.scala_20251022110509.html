How to Play Memory

Game Rules

  • The game consists of pairs of matching cards
  • All cards are placed face down
  • Players take turns flipping two cards at a time
  • If the cards match, the player keeps them and gets another turn
  • If they don't match, the cards are flipped back face down
  • The game ends when all pairs are found
  • The player with the most pairs wins!
